Sciecne Academy Success

The Fraser Heights Integrated Math and Science Academy will be accepting up to 60 Grade 11 students for the 2024-2025 school year. The selection process for this program will involve student grades, references, a practical lab activity and test. Applications for the 2023/2024 Cohort are due: February 15th, 2024. (Please hand into school office by 3pm) Students will be notified of their acceptance into the program by the beginning of March.

Please drop off all completed applications to the front office of Fraser Heights Secondary by Feb 15th, 2024. The office hours are from 7:45 to 4:00 pm during school days.

This year, there will be a Live Virtual Webinar Information Session on February 8th at 7:00pm. The link will be provided on this website on Feburary 6. The join code for the information session is qegfx0d. If you are not from this district, send an email to Mr. Ahn (ahn_j@surreyschools.ca) requesting to be added to the Teams.

After Applying, students will be invited to write a practical test and participate in a practical lab activity.

Note: Pre-requisites:

  • Pre-Calculus 11
  • Pre-Calculus 11
  • Intrinsic passion for science

*Students do not have to have the pre-requisites of Pre-Calculus 11 and Science 10 completed before they apply. They must have it completed before they start the program in September 2023.
